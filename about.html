About Us

Welcome to Roatan Reel Adventures, where passion meets adventure!

Founded by Joevy Bodden, a seasoned angler and expert captain and Guide, Roatan Reel Adventures is dedicated to providing unforgettable fishing experiences and tours on the stunning island of Roatan.

With a deep love for the ocean and a commitment to conservation, Joevy and his team offer a range of fishing trips, including fly fishing, reef fishing, and deep-sea excursions. Whether you're a novice or seasoned angler, we cater to all skill levels and ensure every trip is tailored to your preferences.

Experience the thrill of reeling in your catch amidst Roatan's crystal-clear waters, surrounded by breathtaking scenery and abundant marine life.

Join us for an adventure of a lifetime with Roatan Reel Adventures!
