Fly Fishing Trips

Experience the thrill of fly fishing in the pristine waters of Roatan. Our experienced guides will take you to the best spots for targeting a variety of game fish.

Target Fishes: Bonefish and Permit

Tour to Pigeon Cay and Polo Creek

Embark on a boat tour to Pigeon Cay and Polo Creek, where you can snorkel in the sea, swim with sharks if luck permits, and immerse yourself in the beauty of Roatan's marine life.

Accommodation

Contact us for more information about lodging options. We can help you find the perfect place to stay during your visit to Roatan.

Food Options: Fried Wahoo with Beans and Rice, Tapado, Burgers, Wings
